Wyzant and IXL Math Tutor

Hi, my name is Bill! I graduated summa cum laude from the University of Missouri with a bachelor degree in mathematics. I have a proven record of teaching and tutoring math in middle school and high school with a focus on student success. I have received several school awards for student learning, and I have over fourteen years experience teaching and tutoring math as a certified math teacher.

I am experienced in on-site and remote tutoring using a variety of different learning platforms. My experience includes being a full-time math teacher in Dallas, Texas for middle school math, Algebra 1, Trigonometry, and Pre-calculus. I moved to Missouri after Texas and taught for many years in a high school classroom. I retired from actively instructing students in the classroom and began remotely teaching and tutoring for school districts throughout the United States for several companies that partnered with these schools to supply quality instruction to the students.

Some of the courses that I taught remotely are sixth grade math, Algebra 1, Algebra 2, and Geometry. I have recently partnered with Wyzant and IXL Learning creating Algebra Beagle Tutoring to provide individualized math tutoring to a wide range of students, focusing on student growth and success for the classroom and also supporting the needs of the homeschool student.
